01 — About

A little about me

I'm a multidisciplinary creative with a BA in English literature from The University of British Columbia. I have experience in writing/editing, web development and visual design. My work sits at the intersection of storytelling, technology, and aesthetics — I care about making things that are both well-crafted and meaningful.

Whether I'm drafting a piece of writing, building a web tool, or composing a visual design, I bring the same attention to detail and intention to every project.
